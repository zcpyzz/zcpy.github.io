English

Our Wear OS watch face does not collect any form of user data, including but not limited to personally identifiable information, location data, health data, or other sensitive information.

2. No Data Sharing

Since we do not collect user data, there is no data sharing or selling in any form. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us.

3. Security and Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and have designed our watch face to adhere to the strictest privacy standards to ensure the safety of user data.
